About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Chemistry at Clemson University, working under the supervision of Prof. Marek W. Urban.

My research focuses on the intersection of computational chemistry and materials science, specifically designing next-generation polymers that can heal themselves and respond to environmental stimuli.
